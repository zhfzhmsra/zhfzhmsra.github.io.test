Biography

Fuzheng Zhang is now a Ph.D. Candidate supervised jointly by University of Science and Technology of China and Microsoft Research Asia. His research mainly focuses on consumption and location-based user understanding by using techniques such as machine learning , data mining, big data analysis, etc. He has authored many top-tier international conference papers and journal articles in his research area, such as WWW, Ubicomp, TIST. He has received the best paper award in ICDM2013. He is currently working as a fulltime intern in the Social and Urban Mining (SUM) Group of Microsoft Research Asia. He received his B.S. degrees both in Computer Science and Statistics & Finance from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2010. His research interests include behavior data mining, spatial temporal data mining, ubiquitous computing, recommender system and machine learning.
